Privacy Policy for Pax Dei Archives

This Privacy Policy describes how Pax Dei Archives ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our website.

All intellectual property and assets related to Pax Dei game are reserved by MAINFRAME INDUSTRIES. This site is fan-made and not affiliated with Pax Dei game or MAINFRAME.

Information We Collect

We are committed to collecting only the information necessary for the functionality of our project. The only personal information we collect from our users is your username and email address via Discords's OAUTH process.

How We Use Your Information

The username you provide is used solely for the following purpose:

  • To associate user data with the characters you create on our site, and their associated trades: This allows you to update your character(s) on our site based on in-game activities (trade listings, etc.).

We do not use your username for marketing, analytics, or any other purpose beyond this specific association.

How We Share Your Information

We do not share, sell, or rent your username or any associated data with third parties. Your information is kept private and is only used internally for the stated purpose above.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. While no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, we strive to protect your personal data.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access your information: Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct your information: Ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Request deletion: Request the deletion of your personal information. Please note that deleting your username would remove its association with your character/trade data within our archives.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us
