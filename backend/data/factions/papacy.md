
* **Origin:** Established by **Mellephaneous, the Eighth Redeemer**, to bring order and unity to the Four Kingdoms through the **Petrae Dei**.
* **Nature:** A central religious authority that ruled for over eight centuries, transitioning from a source of peace to a rigid hierarchy defined by **Sloth, Pride, and the suppression of inquiry**.
* **Current Status:** The institution effectively fractured following the death of **Loïos** and the physical cracking of the Petrae Dei, leaving behind a legacy of corruption that contributed to the world’s shattering.
