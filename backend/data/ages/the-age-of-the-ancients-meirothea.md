**Age:** ~350 to ~500

**Redeemer:** Meirothea, the Second Redeemer

**Sins:** Wrath (Moloch), Pride (Lucifer), and Envy (Leviathan)

**Notable Events & Characteristics:**
* Humanity transitioned from mute survival to culture through the gifts of language, song, and symbols
* The transition of the world brought the first Winter, established as a season of reflection rather than mourning
* Meirothea placed the Moon in the sky as a lantern and mirror to unmask beasts twisted by Wrath
* A spiritual war occurred between Meirothea’s followers (including the first magic-wielders, the Fay, and wolves) against Moloch
* Birds entered the world from the realms of Heaven and Fay, drawn by the Redeemer’s voice
* Religious practices shifted toward honoring memory and viewing death as a passage rather than a void
* The age concluded when Meirothea returned to the heavens after being wounded in spirit by the machinations of Pride and Envy
