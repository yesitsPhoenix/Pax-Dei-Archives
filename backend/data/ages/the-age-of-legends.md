**Age:** ~1950 to ~2350

**Redeemer:** Oroael, the Sixth Redeemer

**Sins:** Residual ancient sins manifesting as lingering monsters and warlordism

**Notable Events & Characteristics:**
* A period of slow restoration following the scorched-earth victory of the First Crusade.
* Humanity lived in scattered holds and keeps governed by sword and pact rather than central empires.
* The veil between spirit and flesh remained torn in Haunted Lands, trapping ghosts in the mortal realm.
* Iconic heroes (such as Arthur, Roland, and Siegfried) rose as symbols of sovereignty and sacrifice to hold back the night.
* Oroael arrived as a teacher rather than a warrior, establishing the "Rule of Life" through balance, kindness, and sacred labor.
* The first monasteries and shrines were founded, focusing on healing, music, and scripture to govern human hearts.
* The age concluded with the planting of seeds for future civilizations, moving from survival back toward organized devotion.
