
**Age:** ~1050 to ~1930

**Redeemer:** None (The Age of Man/Divine Silence)

**Sins:** Pride, Retaliation, and Lack of Compassion (manifested as societal policies)

**Notable Events & Characteristics:**
* A thousand-year period where humanity lived without direct divine or angelic guidance as the Fay receded into hiding.
* Four great mortal civilizations rose: Atlantis (West), Thule (North), Helios (East), and Carpathia (Heartlands).
* Atlantis focused on alchemy and living crystal but grew to lack humility.
* Thule preserved ancestral war-chants and strength but became trapped in cycles of retaliation.
* Helios built pyramids of sunstone and valued law but abandoned compassion.
* Carpathia was secretly influenced by a Prince of Hell, leading to a civilization that prioritized perfection and pride.
* The growth of these empires inadvertently opened cracks to the underworld, allowing ancient horrors and the influence of Hell to stir once more.
