Inquisition


  • Alignment: Order
  • Region: High Sanctum
  • Symbol: Burning Eye

The Inquisition stands as a relentless force of order, determined to purge the lands of corruption and chaos. Clad in ceremonial armor and guided by unwavering doctrine, their agents are judge, jury, and executioner in the name of purity.

Founded during the Age of Fracture, they rose to power as society fractured and moral boundaries blurred. The Inquisition stepped in to restore what they deemed as the "True Balance."

Feared and respected, their influence runs deep in the Pax Dei world—some hail them as saviors, others as zealots gone too far.
