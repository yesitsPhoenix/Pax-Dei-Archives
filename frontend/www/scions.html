Scions


  • Alignment: Ancient Knowledge
  • Region: The Forgotten Vaults
  • Symbol: Twin Serpents

The Scions are guardians of ancient lore, descendants of a once-great civilization whose secrets now lie buried beneath the earth.

With a deep reverence for the past, they dedicate their lives to unearthing relics, preserving forbidden texts, and mastering the arcane sciences that others fear.

Their enclaves are hidden, shrouded in illusion and mystery. Some say the Scions remember even the gods long forgotten.
