Zebians


  • Alignment: Elemental Balance
  • Region: Stormreach Coast
  • Symbol: Cresting Wave

The Zebians are elementalists, attuned to the rhythms of tide and storm. Their culture reveres the sea as both a source of life and a force of wrath.

Legends tell of their ancestors forming pacts with water spirits, granting them the ability to summon storms and calm turbulent seas. Zebians embrace this duality—protectors of the coastal settlements, yet fearsome when provoked.

They are often seen as mystical nomads, traveling aboard vessels carved with ancient runes, bringing with them both aid and awe wherever they land.
